Pulled Pork Recipe

Description:

A typical slow cooked dish, but with the instant pot, it's ready to enjoy within 1.5 hours! Amazing

Ingredients

  • 700g Pork
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp caynene
  • 250ml water
  • Your choice of BBQ sauce

Method:

  1. Pat dry the pork
  2. Rub the pork with paprika, salt, garlic/onion powder, and caynne
  3. Turn on Saute on the Instant Pot, wait til it's hot and then brown the meat
  4. Add the water and deglaze
  5. Pressure cook on high for 60 minutes
  6. Let it naturally release for 15 mins
  7. Take out the meat, so you can pour the liquid away
  8. Put the meat back into the Instant Pot, and sauce it up with your favourite BBQ
