Chicken Katsu

This is a japanese cuisine. It has panko chicken alongside a lovely curry sauce served over rice

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Tumeric
  • Curry Powder
  • Stock Cube
  • Coconut Milk
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Saute onion and garlic, then add the spices to toast
  2. Add chicken stock
  3. Slowly add coconut milk, bring it to boil each time
  4. Add the sugar and soy sauce
  5. Blend it all together
  6. Flour -> Egg -> Panko
  7. Deep fry or Air fry at 205o for 16 mins
