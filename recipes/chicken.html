Chicken Burrito Bite Size Pieces

Description:

This chicken recipe is filled with flavour, best served in a wrap or with salad

Ingredients List:

  • 900g boneless chicken thigh
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 205
  2. Combine the spices and rub on the chicken
  3. Sear the chicken on both side. This will take around 2 mins each side. (NOTE. Chicken is still raw at this point)
  4. Cook in the oven for 10-15 minutes
  5. Take the chicken out, and dice them up
  6. Toss back into the tray you used to cook for maximum flavour
  7. Serve in a wrap or a salad
