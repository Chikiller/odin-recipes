Chicken Burrito

This recipe has cubed chicken, with a lot of freedom as to what fillings you want

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Spices
    • Paprika
    • Garlic Powder
    • Cumin
    • Onion Powder
    • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix all the spices together and marinate the chicken overnight
  2. Preheat the oven to 205o celcius
  3. Heat up a bit of oil and sear the chicken on both sides
  4. Cook the chicken in the oven for 10 minutes
  5. Take it out the oven, cube up the chicken and put it back
  6. Toss into the juices for max flavour and continue cooking for 5 mins
  7. Enjoy with fillings of your choice. I recommend sweet chilli, lettuce, sweetcorn and fried onions
