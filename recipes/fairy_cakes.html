Fairy Cakes Recipe

Description

The nostalgic fairy cakes, one of the first things any baker makes. Small bite sized pieces, easy to demolish.

Ingredients:

  • 125g Self Raising Flour
  • 125 Sugar
  • 125 Butter
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tbsp milk

Method:

  1. Add the flour sugar and butter and whisk away
  2. Add the 2 eggs and whisk more
  3. Add the baking powder, vanilla extract and milk and whisk further
  4. Use a baking tray and split evenly into 12. Maximum is about 3/4 as they rise
  5. Bake eat 180 for 10 minutes
